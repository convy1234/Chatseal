Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ChatSeal (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Facebook-connected application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook account or Page, ChatSeal may receive the following information through the Facebook Graph API, depending on your permissions:

  • Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Facebook Page information (Page name, ID, profile photo)
  • Messages and comments sent to your Page
  • Posts created or scheduled through ChatSeal

3. How We Use This Information

  • To display, manage, and respond to Page messages and comments
  • To allow you to create and publish posts on your Page
  • To maintain secure authentication with your Facebook account
  • To improve service quality and customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and user data are stored securely using encryption. Tokens are used only to communicate with the Facebook Graph API on your behalf. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

ChatSeal does not share your Facebook data except where required by law or to comply with Facebook’s Platform Policy.

6. User Control

You can revoke ChatSeal’s access to your Facebook account or Page at any time via your Facebook settings:

https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools

7. Children’s Privacy

ChatSeal is not directed toward individuals under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
